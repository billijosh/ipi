Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our programs and services

What are your admission requirements?

For language courses, no prior experience is required. For professional courses, basic computer literacy is recommended. International students may need to provide additional documentation.

Do you offer financial aid or payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for all our courses. Some programs also qualify for government skills development funding. Contact our admissions team for details.

How do I know which course is right for me?

We offer free career counseling sessions to help you choose the best program based on your goals, experience level, and schedule. Book a consultation with our academic advisor.

Do you offer corporate training for organizations?

Absolutely! We provide customized training solutions for businesses, including group discounts, on-site training, and tailored programs. Contact our corporate training department.
