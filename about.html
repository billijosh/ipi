About Infoworld Professional Institute

Discover our story, mission, and what makes us the preferred choice for professional training in Ghana.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, accessible training that empowers individuals with practical skills for personal development and professional success in a globalized world.

Our Vision

To become West Africa's leading training institution, recognized for excellence in language and professional skills development that transforms lives and communities.

Our History

From humble beginnings to becoming a leader in professional education

2010

Foundation

Infoworld Professional Institute was founded by Dr. Kofi Ansah as a small language center offering English classes to international students.

2013

First Expansion

Added French language program and moved to a larger facility in central Accra to accommodate growing student numbers.

2016

Professional Courses

Launched our first professional training programs in Microsoft Office and basic marketing skills.

2019

Accreditation

Received full accreditation from the Ghana Education Service and expanded our digital marketing program.

2023

Modern Campus

Opened our state-of-the-art training facility with smart classrooms and student support services.

Accreditation & Partnerships
