How to Apply

Our simple 4-step process makes it easy to join our programs

1

Choose Your Course

Browse our course catalog and select the program that matches your goals. Need help deciding? Contact our advisors.

2

Complete Application

Fill out our online application form below or visit our campus to apply in person.

3

Submit Documents

For certain programs, you may need to provide additional documents (ID, previous certificates). Our team will guide you.

4

Complete Registration

Pay your tuition fees and receive your course materials and schedule to begin classes.
